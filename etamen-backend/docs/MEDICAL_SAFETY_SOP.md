# Medical Safety SOP

This SOP applies to Etamen patient, provider, support, admin, AI, radiology, lab, gym, and nutrition experiences.

## Core Rules

- AI cannot diagnose.
- AI cannot prescribe medication.
- AI cannot tell a patient to stop medication.
- AI cannot replace a doctor, emergency service, or clinical judgment.
- Support staff cannot provide diagnosis, treatment, or interpretation of clinical results.
- Lab and radiology result UI must show metadata/results only and must not interpret results medically.
- Nutrition and gym content must remain wellness/operations oriented unless reviewed and approved by qualified providers.

## Red Flags

Any report of severe symptoms, immediate danger, chest pain, breathing trouble, stroke signs, severe allergic reaction, suicidal ideation, loss of consciousness, uncontrolled bleeding, or pregnancy emergency must be redirected to emergency care.

## Emergency Disclaimer Copy

Arabic:

```text
لو الحالة طارئة أو فيها خطر فوري، اتصل بالإسعاف أو توجه لأقرب طوارئ فورًا. اطمن لا يغني عن الطبيب أو خدمات الطوارئ.
```

English:

```text
If this is an emergency or immediate risk, call emergency services or go to the nearest emergency department now. Etamen does not replace a doctor or emergency services.
```

## Support Handling

- Use neutral operational wording.
- Do not say a result is normal or abnormal.
- Do not recommend treatment.
- Route clinical questions to a doctor/provider visit.
- Escalate safety concerns using the support `medical_safety` category.
