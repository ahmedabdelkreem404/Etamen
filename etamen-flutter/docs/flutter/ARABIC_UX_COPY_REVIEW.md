## Intentionally Kept Formal

These remain formal because they are safety/legal copy:

- Medical disclaimer.
- AI disclaimer.
- Medication disclaimer.
- Care plan progress disclaimer.
- Refund/cancellation draft policy.
- Emergency/red-flag AI guidance.

## Copy Principles Used

- Use patient-friendly Arabic for normal flow.
- Avoid panic language in health modules.
- Keep backend authority clear for payments/status.
- Do not claim diagnosis, treatment, cure, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid technical labels in normal UI where possible.

## Remaining Copy Needing Review

- Some older Sprint docs and labels may still need full Arabic editorial pass.
- English copy is functional but should receive a native-quality review before public launch.
- Legal text remains draft and requires legal review.
